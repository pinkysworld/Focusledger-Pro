Introduction

Focusledger ("we," "our," or "the app") is a productivity tracking application for macOS that helps you understand how you spend your time and improve your work habits. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

This Privacy Policy explains what data Focusledger collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Data Collection & Storage

Focusledger collects and stores the following data locally on your Mac:

Data Type Description Storage
Activity Tracking App names, window titles, time spent, categories Local Only
Focus Sessions Session times, goals, focus scores Local Only
Screenshots Optional periodic screenshots (if enabled) Local Only
Input Activity Keystroke counts, mouse clicks (NOT actual keys) Local Only
Journal & Notes Your written entries and AI summaries Local Only
Preferences App settings and configurations Local Only

Third-Party Services

Apple Intelligence

Our AI features use Apple Intelligence, which processes all data entirely on your device. Apple does not receive your productivity data through this feature.

Apple StoreKit

For in-app purchases, Apple processes transactions according to their privacy policy. We receive purchase status and subscription validity, but NOT payment details or Apple ID information.

No Analytics Services

We do NOT use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any similar third-party analytics services. We do not track your app usage for marketing purposes.

Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • macOS App Sandbox: Focusledger runs in a sandboxed environment with limited system access
  • Local Storage Only: Data never leaves your device over the network
  • System Keychain: Sensitive preferences use the macOS Keychain
  • FileVault Encryption: All stored data benefits from macOS disk encryption

Your Rights & Controls

  • Access: View all tracked data in the Timeline and Insights views
  • Export: Export your complete data history in JSON, PDF, HTML, or CSV formats
  • Delete: Delete individual sessions, date ranges, or all data with one click
  • Control: Enable/disable tracking, exclude specific apps, use privacy mode
  • Retention: Configure automatic deletion after 30, 90, 180 days, or 1 year

Children's Privacy

Focusledger is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
